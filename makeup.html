PINK FAIRY

iThis look features dreamy pastel pink eyeshadow blended across the lids and into the cheeks, giving a soft flush of color.

BLUE FAIRY

A blue fairy makeup look is cool, mystical, and otherworldly — like a magical creature from a moonlit forest or icy realm. It features soft to bold shades of blue eyeshadow, often blended with silver, white, or lavender for an ethereal glow.

MUMMY

A drag mummy with white eyes slays the undead runway with a bone-chilling yet glamorous twist. Pale, cracked skin is sculpted with deep contour to create sunken cheekbones and temple hollows, giving that ancient, cursed look.

DRAGLOOK

Drag makeup is a transformative art form that turns faces into full fantasy. It starts with a flawless, full-coverage base, often using cream foundation and heavy-duty powders to create a blank canvas. .

BLOODY

bloody makeup brings the horror fantasy to life — dripping red FX blood from the mouth, scratches across the chest, or a slit throat illusion. The mix of gore and glam is iconic, especially with a bold red lip or blood-stained highlighter.

BUBBLES

This look features pastel or iridescent shades with 3D bubble-like designs painted or attached to the face. Think glossy skin, pearl accents, and soap-like shimmer floating around the eyes or cheeks. It’s fantasy-meets-freaky — like a haunted bubble queen from a magical dimension.
